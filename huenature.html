🌸 About Us

Hue Nature was started on 14th March 2026 with a simple idea to make environmental action and everyday awareness go hand in hand 🌿

We focus on two things: taking real steps for the environment and helping people understand what they use in their daily lives. From planting trees 🌳 and organizing clean-up drives 🧹 to talking about the hidden chemicals in products like soaps, perfumes, cosmetics, and more 🧴🧪 — everything we do is about making small but meaningful changes. Not just that, but we also give the alternatives for all these harmful chemicals!

We also conduct eco-friendly workshops, collaborations, and seminars in schools 🏫, cafes ☕, and community spaces to make sustainability more interactive and easy to connect with ✨🌼

🚫 The Toxic Cabinet

Your daily routine may include hidden chemicals 🧪 — YOU DEFINITELY NEED TO STOP USING THEM NOW!

  • 🧼 Soaps: SLS, Triclosan
  • 💨 Deodorants: Aluminum, Phthalates
  • 💅 Nail Paints: DBP, Toluene, Formaldehyde
  • 💄 Cosmetics: Lead, Carbon Black, PFAS
  • 🧴 Lotions: Parabens, Mineral Oils

🌸 About the Founder

Hasitha Chowdary Nalajala ✨

The initiative was built around a simple but often overlooked idea — that caring for the environment goes beyond large actions and begins with everyday choices. From the products people use daily to the spaces they live in, small decisions collectively shape a larger impact 🧴🌍

Through activities like tree plantation drives 🌳, community clean-up initiatives 🧹, and interactive workshops in schools, cafes, and local spaces ☕🏫, Hue Nature focuses on making sustainability more approachable and practical.

Alongside environmental action, the platform also highlights awareness about commonly used products and the chemicals they contain 🧪 — encouraging more informed and conscious choices without making it complicated.

Hue Nature reflects a balanced approach combining action with awareness, and simplicity with purpose 🌼
